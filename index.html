Recovery Zone

Track your recovery to optimize performance.
Breathe in... Hold... Breathe out...

Recovery is the engine that turns hard work into long-term gains. Your muscles rebuild, your nervous system resets, and your heart repairs after intense sessions.

  • Sleep: Deep rest supports hormone balance, muscle repair, and energy restoration.
  • Nutrition: Protein, electrolytes, and healthy carbs refill fuel and support recovery.
  • Active recovery: Light movement, stretching, and walking improve blood flow.
  • Mindset: Recovery days are not lazy days—they are growth days.
Tip: aim for 7–9 hours of sleep, hydrate consistently, and include low-impact mobility on rest days.
