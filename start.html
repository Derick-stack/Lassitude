🚀 Welcome to Lassitude

Your comprehensive guide to faith-driven personal development, fitness, and growth. Follow these steps to get started!

📋 Getting Started Guide

1

🔐 Create Your Account

Sign up for a free account to unlock all features:

  • Click "Login" in the navigation bar
  • Select "Sign Up" tab
  • Enter username, email, and phone (optional for SMS recovery)
  • Create a strong password
  • Complete registration to access your personalized dashboard
💡 Tip: Provide both email and phone for maximum password recovery options.
2

⚙️ Customize Your Experience

Make Lassitude yours with these settings:

  • Themes: Choose from Midnight Gold, Rose Twilight, Deep Ocean, or Emerald Night
  • Profile: Set your display name, bio, and profile picture (emoji)
  • Light Mode: Toggle between dark and light themes
  • Notifications: Control daily quotes, streak reminders, and achievements

Access all settings through the "Settings" link in the navigation bar.

3

🏠 Using the Main Dashboard

Your central hub for daily growth:

  • Motivation Card: Get daily inspirational quotes from Quotable API
  • Daily Scripture: Receive random Bible verses for spiritual guidance
  • Workout Generator: Discover new exercises with target muscles and equipment
  • Focus Timer: Use Pomodoro technique (25-min work sessions with breaks)
  • Music Library: Access curated Spotify playlists for different activities
  • Video Library: Watch guided workouts and training videos
  • Community Feed: Share progress and connect with others
  • NADIR AI: Get personalized advice on fitness, faith, and growth

Access the main dashboard: Click here to go to Home

4

📊 Tracking Your Progress

Monitor your growth journey:

  • XP & Levels: Earn experience points through completed tasks and workouts
  • Streaks: Build consecutive day streaks for consistency
  • Progress Page: View detailed statistics and achievements
  • Meal Tracking: Log nutrition and get meal suggestions
  • Hydration: Track daily water intake
  • Workout Logging: Record completed exercises and sessions

Visit the "Progress" page to see your complete growth analytics.

5

🎯 Focus & Productivity Tools

Maximize your concentration:

  • Pomodoro Timer: 25-minute focused work sessions with 5-minute breaks
  • Custom Timer: Set any duration for specific tasks
  • Guided Workouts: Follow structured strength, HIIT, or yoga sessions
  • Meditation: Quick 1-minute centering exercises
  • Affirmations: Daily positive statements for mindset
  • Video Challenges: 15-second stretch, prayer, or breathing exercises

Access these tools through the "Focus" page or expand cards on the main dashboard.

6

🔮 Planning Your Future

Build long-term success:

  • Goal Setting: Define specific, measurable objectives
  • Habit Building: Create sustainable daily routines
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor long-term growth trends
  • Milestone Planning: Set checkpoints for major achievements
  • Reflection: Review progress and adjust strategies

Use the "Future" page to plan your path to success.

7

🛠️ Advanced Features

Power user tools:

  • Global Search: Find content, quotes, workouts across the entire app
  • GitHub Integration: Connect your coding projects and track contributions
  • Email Notifications: Subscribe for updates and motivational content
  • Data Export: Download your complete data for backup
  • AI Assistant: Get personalized advice from NADIR AI
  • Community Features: Share achievements and get inspired by others
8

🔐 Security & Privacy

Your data is protected:

  • Password Recovery: Reset via email or SMS (if provided)
  • Secure Storage: All data stored locally in your browser
  • Privacy First: No personal data shared without permission
  • Account Control: Change password or delete account anytime
  • Data Export: Download and backup your information
🔒 Security Note: For maximum security, use a strong password and enable two-factor recovery with both email and phone.
